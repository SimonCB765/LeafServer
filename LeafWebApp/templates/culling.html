Culling a subset of the PDB allows you to select criteria (such as resolution, R-value, maximum permissible sequence identity, etc.) to use in culling, and enables you to specify a subset of the PDB that you want to perform the culling on. The subset can be generated from user uploaded chains/entries, or all the chains/entries of a specified organism.

Culling user submitted sequences allows you to upload protein sequences in FASTA format, and specifiy the maximum permissible sequence identity and minimum and maxium values for the protein sequence length.
